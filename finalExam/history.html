Origins

The Kansas City Royals were established in 1969 as an expansion team, quickly becoming a cornerstone of Midwest baseball.

Milestones

  2. 1985: First World Series Championship
  3. 2014: Return to the World Series
  4. 2015: Second World Series Championship
