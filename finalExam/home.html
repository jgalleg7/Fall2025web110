Welcome to Royals Baseball

The Kansas City Royals are a Major League Baseball team founded in 1969. Known for their passionate fan base and memorable championship runs, the Royals embody the spirit of Kansas City.

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1969
  • Home Stadium: Kauffman Stadium
  • World Series Titles: 1985, 2015
  • Team Colors: Royal Blue & White
  • Division: American League Central
